Why do you want to join Texas Bluebonnets?

I want to find my family! As a CS major, I am around mostly men, and I experienced what I think is extreme imposter syndrome during my freshman year. A lot of times, I felt like I was more in competition with people I met than making meaningful connections, so I am seeking a genuine community where I can be unapologetically myself :)

How do you embody Service?

I value service a lot! I have a local Vietnamese temple near my family home that feeds anyone that joins on Sundays. During my summers, I go to temple every Sunday morning to help out with food preparation, cleaning, and plant care for the nuns that live there. I go with my mom and my sister, so it is not only a way to bond with my family, but also a time completely away from screens and focused on giving back.

The Pillar of Service

I value service a lot! I have a local Vietnamese temple near my family home that feeds anyone that joins on Sundays. During my summers, I go to temple every Sunday morning to help out with food preparation, cleaning, and plant care for the nuns that live there. I go with my mom and my sister, so it is not only a way to bond with my family, but also a time completely away from screens and focused on giving back. It became something I looked forward to every weekend as it allowed me to connect with my community while quietly reflecting on my own gratitude for all of the blessings in my life. Because I have so many privileges in my life, such as getting to go to university, being able to move my body, and having access to warm meals, I strongly believe that taking time to help out anybody is essential.

"Sunday Mornings at Temple" - Mixed media on gratitude and community, 2024

Balance & Running

I started running consistently about a year ago, and I believe that it has altered the trajectory on how I view life. It has taught me how to eat properly, get excited to challenge myself, and the importance of consistency. I am most passionate about balance in my life right now. I focus on how I feel and allow myself breaks while also pushing myself when I feel it is needed.

"The Runner's Philosophy" - Movement study, 2024

My Little Pony Manifesto

I could write a novel on My Little Pony lore and how the franchise impacts the world's politics, philosophy, and culture. I am unironically rewatching My Little Pony, and I think rewatching it as a 19 year old is such a refreshing and educative experience. Each of the main six ponies embodies emotions and personality traits that so many people can relate to.
